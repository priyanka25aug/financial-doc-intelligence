ACME Financial Corporation — Q3 2024 Earnings Call Transcript
October 15, 2024

CEO Remarks:

Good morning, everyone. I'm pleased to report that Q3 2024 was an exceptional quarter for ACME Financial.

Revenue came in at $4.2 billion, representing a 12% year-over-year increase and beating analyst consensus by 5%. Earnings per share (EPS) of $1.87 exceeded the consensus estimate of $1.72, driven by strong performance in our institutional trading division.

We are raising our full-year revenue guidance to $16.5 billion from $15.8 billion. We expect Q4 EPS in the range of $1.90 to $2.00, reflecting continued momentum in asset management and advisory services.

CFO Commentary:

Operating margins improved to 34%, up 200 basis points versus Q3 2023. Net interest income increased by $450 million to $2.1 billion. We maintained a Tier 1 capital ratio of 13.2%, well above the 10.5% regulatory minimum.

Looking forward, management's fiscal 2024 full-year EPS guidance of $7.40 to $7.60 reflects our confidence in sustained revenue growth.
